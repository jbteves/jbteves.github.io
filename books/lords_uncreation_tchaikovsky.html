The Lords of Uncreation

Adrian Tchaikovsky

This was an epic conclusion to Adrian Tchaikovsky's "The Final Architecture" series. Though strange at turns, it brilliantly concludes this magnificient space opera. I enjoyed the pacing, the plot twists and turns, and felt that the resolution was unimpeachable. My hopes going in were high and Tchaikovsky managed to exceed them. A really excellent series to the end.
